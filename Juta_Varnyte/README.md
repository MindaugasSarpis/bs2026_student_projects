## Project for Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics course - Wind Farm Design & Energy Analysis Tool

This dashboard shows how wind conditions and different turbine models affect energy production at a site. It uses real wind data to display wind speeds, directions, monthly patterns, turbine power curves, and estimated yearly energy output. You can compare turbines, adjust heights, and see how wake effects change performance when turbines are placed in rows. The goal is to give a clear, visual overview of how a wind farm might behave under different conditions.
